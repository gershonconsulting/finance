MRR Logic in Use

Option A — Simple Actual Billing MRR: MRR = total value of all invoices issued in the current month. This reflects true monthly invoicing activity rather than an average. Feb and March are treated independently. ARR = MRR × 12 based on this month's actual billing.

Data Definitions

Active client (monthly): unique client with at least one invoice published in that month
Total invoiced (monthly): sum of all invoices issued in that month
Paid invoice: invoice fully paid (Status = PAID)
Unpaid invoice: invoice not fully paid
Late invoice: unpaid invoice past due date
Client lifetime: first invoice month to latest invoice month, inclusive
Billed months: count of distinct months with at least one invoice
Collection rate: amount paid ÷ amount invoiced in that month
