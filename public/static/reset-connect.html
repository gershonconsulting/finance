Reset & Connect to Xero

Fix authentication errors and connect with default credentials

This page will:

  • • Clear any incorrect credentials from your browser
  • • Use the default working credentials
  • • Connect directly to Xero OAuth

What's Happening?

You're seeing "invalid_client" errors because the Client Secret in your browser's Settings doesn't match what Xero expects.

This page bypasses your saved settings and uses the default working credentials that are pre-configured in the application.

After clicking "Clear Settings & Connect": You'll be redirected to Xero's login page, authorize the app, and be connected with real data!
