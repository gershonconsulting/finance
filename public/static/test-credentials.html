The credentials in .dev.vars are NOT working!

This means the Client Secret has changed or is incorrect. You need to generate a NEW Client Secret in your Xero app.

Step 1: Generate New Client Secret

  1. 1 Go to https://developer.xero.com/myapps
  2. 2 Open your app (Client ID ending in E6)
  3. 3 Scroll to "Client secret 1" and click "Generate another secret"
  4. 4 IMMEDIATELY COPY the secret (shown only once!)

Step 2: Test Your Credentials

What This Page Does

  • • Tests if your credentials work with Xero OAuth
  • • Uses the POST /auth/login endpoint to test token exchange
  • • If successful, connects you to Xero with real data
  • • If it fails, shows you the exact error from Xero
